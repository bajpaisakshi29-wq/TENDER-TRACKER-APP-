# TENDER-TRACKER-APP-
Tender Tracker is a smart application that helps the company organize, monitor, and manage tenders efficiently by tracking deadlines, statuses, and important details in one centralized dashboard.

The Tender Tracker Application is a comprehensive digital solution designed to simplify and streamline the complete tender management process for organizations. It serves as a centralized platform where every tender can be recorded, monitored, updated, and managed from the moment it is identified until the project is completed. Instead of relying on spreadsheets, emails, and manual follow-ups, the application brings all tender-related information into a single, organized dashboard, enabling teams to work more efficiently and make informed decisions.

The application begins by allowing users to create a new tender entry with essential details such as the tender reference number, client name, organization, submission deadline, estimated value, responsible team member, and current status. Each tender is assigned a unique record, making it easy to search, filter, and access whenever required.

One of the application's key features is its intelligent notification and reminder system. Automated reminders notify users about important upcoming deadlines, including document submission dates, bid opening dates, payment due dates, security deposit deadlines, sample submission schedules, and contract-related milestones. These notifications help ensure that no critical task is overlooked and significantly reduce the risk of missed opportunities due to human error.

The Tender Tracker also provides real-time status tracking throughout the tender lifecycle. Every stage, such as Tender Identified, Documents Collected, Bid Preparation, Submitted, Under Evaluation, Awarded, Lost, Purchase Order Received, Production, Dispatch, and Completed, can be updated within the application. This allows management to instantly understand the progress of every active tender without manually contacting different departments.

A dedicated payment management module enables users to record and monitor all financial transactions associated with a tender. Information such as Earnest Money Deposits (EMD), security deposits, performance guarantees, invoice payments, pending receivables, payment schedules, and other financial records can be maintained in one place. This helps the company track outstanding payments, avoid delays, and maintain accurate financial records for every project.

The application also supports secure document management. Users can upload and store important files such as tender documents, technical specifications, BOQs, compliance certificates, drawings, purchase orders, quotations, invoices, correspondence, agreements, inspection reports, and other supporting documents directly within the tender record. This eliminates the need to search through multiple folders or emails and ensures that all relevant information is readily available whenever required.

Powerful search and filtering capabilities allow users to locate tenders based on client, organization, status, submission date, project value, assigned employee, or reference number. Archived tenders remain accessible for future reference, audits, reporting, and analysis while keeping the active dashboard clean and organized.

Once a tender has been successfully completed or closed, it is automatically moved to the Archive section. Archived tenders preserve their complete history, including status updates, uploaded documents, payment records, and communications. This historical database enables the company to analyze past performance, prepare for similar future tenders, and maintain proper documentation for compliance purposes.

By digitizing the entire tender management workflow, the Tender Tracker Application improves productivity, enhances collaboration among departments, minimizes manual errors, and provides complete visibility into every tender. It helps organizations save time, monitor payments efficiently, manage documents securely, receive timely reminders, and maintain an accurate record of all completed and ongoing tenders, making it an essential tool for efficient business operations.
